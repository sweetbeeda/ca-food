about us

Welcome to CA Group, a family-owned business rooted in tradition and driven by innovation. Established in 1971 in Coimbatore, our journey began with CA Store, a grocery departmental wholesale store that has been serving the community for over five decades. Today, we proudly continue the legacy of quality and trust across multiple ventures, while embracing the evolving needs of our customers.

At CA Food , we bring a unique and authentic taste of South India to your doorstep through our food delivery and take-away services. With over 20 years of catering experience, we specialize in curating flavorful and nutritious meals, designed to bring families and communities together. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond food to our proprietary brand, CA Masala, where we manufacture and distribute a wide variety of high-quality South Indian masalas, ensuring every dish you make is rich in tradition and taste.

To make your special occasions memorable, we also offer CA Party Hall, a versatile venue perfect for hosting events, parties, and celebrations of all kinds. Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional service and creating unforgettable experiences for you and your guests.
